    About us

    Hope Kitchen, a nonprofit organization dedicated to combating food waste, has strong local partnerships with major supermarkets and distributors in its region. Our mission is to support the community by minimizing food waste while promoting a "pay as you feel" service, offering a daily menu crafted from freshly diverted ingredients.

    Through our collaborations with food distributors in the area, Hope Kitchen has introduced food boxes containing fresh food sourced from grocery stores and farm markets. These boxes are primarily allocated to students, families, and especially our volunteers, given the limited availability of this resource. To facilitate access, we have implemented a reservation system for those eligible to receive weekly food boxes.

    At Hope Kitchen, we take pride in extending a warm welcome to our community, providing nourishment and hope to those facing food crisis.

    Hope Kitchen Objectives

    We are dedicated on these points to make a difference in our community

    1.

    Sustainable food

    Reducing food waste and diverting it into delicious nutritious food for the community

    2.

    Educating community

    Raising awareness about food is essential to avoid starvation, food waste and also to address its importance in contemporary society

    3.

    Create hope

    Through our steadfast commitment to reducing food waste, we aspire to instil hope among the less fortunate, ensuring their sustenance while fostering enduring brand loyalty

    4.

    Reaching our community

    As our community expands rapidly, we endeavour to amplify advocacy against food waste through the strategic use of social media platforms and bus shelters across the area
